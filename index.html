About Me

I am Yijun Xiao, an urban enthusiast with a M.Eng. focusing on spatial data science, with work expierence at a leading urban developer in China. I explore built environments through big data, with a focus on consumption geography and built environment morphology.

I obtained my master's degree from Tianjin University, Before that, I got my B.Arch. degree from Wuhan University, Currently, I work at the planning and development team at Shui On Land, based in Shanghai

Research Interests

My research focuses on Urban Science, particularly in the areas of Consumption Geography , Built Environment Morphology , and Urban Big Data Analysis and Visualization .
