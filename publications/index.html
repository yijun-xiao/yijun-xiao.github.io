Analysing the Factors in Shaping Neighbourhood Restaurants Sceneries

Yijun Xiao*, Sinan Yuan.

IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV), 2023.

 [Abstract] [Paper] [Bibtex]

This study looks at neighborhood restaurants in Tianjin, China, during rapid urbanization and changing consumer preferences. It analyzes factors like demand, transportation, and competition, finding a significant rise in community restaurants from 2018 to 2021. Key influences include population density and housing prices.
